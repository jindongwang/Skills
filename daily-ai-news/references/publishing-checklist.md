# Publishing Checklist

Use this checklist immediately before writing to Notion or Slack.

- The report date is the run date in Asia/Shanghai.
- The story list is deduplicated; one story has one source link.
- The source set includes global and Chinese coverage where available.
- Items emphasize AI, major tech companies, semiconductors, infrastructure, markets, funding, or finance.
- English and Chinese versions describe the same story set.
- Every item is exactly a headline plus a link.
- Items do not include summaries, commentary, source labels outside links, timestamps, tags, or extra metadata.
- Notion receives only the Chinese version under the run-date section.
- Slack receives one message with both language sections.
- Automation memory is updated after publishing.
